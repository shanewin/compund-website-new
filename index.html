About The Compound
Rooted in Bushwick. Elevated by Design.

Welcome to The Compound — two beautifully designed buildings offering modern luxury apartments in Brooklyn's most vibrant neighborhood. Located on Bushwick Avenue, just steps from buzzing cafés, street art corridors, and the L train, The Compound blends elevated design with industrial soul.

Each apartment features premium finishes, spacious layouts, and curated interiors by Input Creative Studio — from custom millwork and stone countertops to chef-grade appliances. Experience the perfect blend of comfort, style, and community in a space crafted for modern city living.

Amenities

01

Rooftop Terrace

Escape to the sky on our rooftop terrace—a beautifully designed urban retreat with panoramic views of the Brooklyn and Manhattan skylines. Enjoy professional-grade grilling stations, cozy lounge seating, and unobstructed vistas perfect for morning coffee or evening cocktails. Whether you're hosting friends or unwinding solo, this is city living elevated.

02

Resident's Lounge

The resident's lounge blends industrial warmth with refined design—featuring exposed brick walls, custom wood trim, and gallery-inspired artwork that brings each corner to life. Recessed LED lighting casts a soft ambient glow, highlighting premium finishes like matte porcelain tile and curated accent details. It's a relaxed, elegant space made for quiet mornings, casual meetups, or creative work sessions.
